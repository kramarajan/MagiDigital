Who Am I?



I'm Bharathi Raja

Commercial and fashion photographer, traveler, nature enthusiast and die-hard dreamer. Studied commerce at the madras university, towards which I still have a passion for, but I have turned to photography early on and yes, that was very much before the pixels and Instagram.

FUNDAMENTALS

Classic beauty, class, character, romantic reality, natural light, black and whites and vivid colors. I like to say that my pictures are honest. Simplicity, mixed with intimacy, elegance and energy. I undertake assignments in advertising photography, industrial photography, architecture photography, interiors & exterior photography, fashion photography, model portfolios, food photography and panoramic photography.
